Course Overview

Master in Business Administration (MBA) program is designed to develop the skills required for careers in business and management. The role of an MBA is to teach management principles and practices that help improve organizational efficiency and effectiveness.

Description

Our MBA program offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers essential business concepts, analytical tools, and leadership skills. The program is designed for both fresh graduates and working professionals looking to advance their careers.

Application Procedure

Required Documents

Previous Education Certificates

Previous Education Certificates (Bachelor's degree/PGD/diploma) & transcripts in PDF format.

  • Bachelor's degree in any field with minimum 1 year work experience
  • Post Graduate Diploma with several years of work experience
  • Graduate Diploma with 5-8 years of work experience
Professional Experience

Experience letters from current and previous employers stating:

  • Position held and duration
  • Roles and responsibilities
  • Company details and contact information
Personal Documents
  • Updated CV/Resume
  • Passport size photograph
  • Copy of valid ID/Passport
  • Statement of Purpose
